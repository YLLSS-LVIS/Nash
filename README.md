# Nash
## **WARNING**
LVIS Nash *does not* operate with fiat currency or regulated assets, including cryptocurrencies. No path of conversion to the abovementioned assets are offered. Though LVIS Nash is a sophisticated simulation of an actual financial exchange, it shall not be used to constue any advice on investment or trading decisions. Trading in financial markets in real life can lead to losses exceeding one's initial capital. DYOR.

The Nash codebase is intended for public auditing and research on the LVIS Nash exchange. Due to legal reasons, please *do not deploy any software in this repository with real money.*

## About Nash
Nash is the official exchange in the LVIS for bounded-resolution, fully-collateralised, cash-settled futures contracts, more commonly known as event contracts, prediction contracts, or binary options.
